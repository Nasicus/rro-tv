RRO TV (unofficial) — Privacy Policy

This Google TV / Android TV app is an unofficial, open-source client for Radio Rottu Oberwallis. It is not affiliated with RRO or Pomona.

Data we collect

None. The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, device identifiers, analytics, or crash reports. There are no user accounts, no logins, and no tracking of any kind.

Network requests the app makes

  • The audio stream URL of the channel you choose to play (streaming.rro.ch).
  • A JSON query to the public playlist API (rro.playlist-api.deliver.media) once per track change, to display the current song title and artist.

Both endpoints are operated by third parties (Pomona / Deliver Media); their respective privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

Permissions

The app requests only the permissions required to stream audio: network access and the standard foreground-service permission for background playback.
